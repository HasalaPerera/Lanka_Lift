What is Lanka Lift


There are many taxi-hailing services in Sri Lanka at the moment however there are no proper services that connect people who have long-term vehicle requirements with vehicle-renting individuals. We are going to focus on those people and connect them by using the platform. Our solution could be helpful for foreigners to avoid inconvenient situations that they face when finding a vehicle for rent. This service is not limited to foreigners, Sri Lankans also have such transportation requirements for leisure and business-related activities, therefore they can also fulfil their vehicle requirements using our system.

FAQ questions

To make a reservation, simply visit our website or mobile app. Select your desired pickup and drop-off locations, choose the vehicle type, specify the rental dates and times, and proceed to confirm your booking. You can also contact our customer service for assistance in making a reservation.

To rent a vehicle, you typically need a valid driver's license, a major credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. The driver's license must be current and meet the minimum age requirements for the specific vehicle class. Additionally, some locations may require additional identification or documentation, so it's advisable to check the specific requirements for your rental location.

In the event of an accident, first, ensure the safety of all parties involved. Contact local authorities and then inform our customer service as soon as possible.
